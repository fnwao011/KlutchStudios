ABOUT US

We are a recording studio built by artists for artists. We understand the gripes that most modern artists find when it comes to musical production. We were founded in 2001 by former head engineer Bill Russell. He started out as a folk artist and later transitioned into mixing and mastering. With his knowledge of production, he was able to work with a range of artists regardless of genre. He pushed for the recording of music because he believed it was kin to storytelling, and that when it is all said and done our words will live on forever. SERVICES

Audio Recording
We do all kinds of vocal recordings, whether its for music or voice acting. This service also comes with a vast selection of microphone choices during the recording session. Base price starts at 50$/hour
Audio Mixing
Audio mixing is the process of optimizing and combining multitrack(instruments) recordings and audio recordings into a final product. The engineer you choose to work with will take the raw recordings and transform them into whatever ideas you had. Base price is 50$/hour.
Audio Mastering
Audio mixing is the process of preparing and transferring mixed recorded audio to its final polished version. This involves making making the song clear and loud enough so it sounds good on whatever form of audio player and streaming service. Base price is 60$/hour.
